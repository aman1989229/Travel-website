The scenic Promenade Beach in Pondicherry is one of the favorite destination of family beach vacations. The cleanliness of the beach and the lofty boulders spread across the shore present a charming picture.

Things to do: Strolling on the beach and clicking pictures at sunset

Places to stay: Le Pondy, The Wildflower Resort & Spa, and Le Dupleix – A Sarovar Hotel
