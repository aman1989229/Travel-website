The scenic Palolem beach is one of the best places for family beach vacations in South Goa. Its serenity, nearly transparent waters, plush green coconut plantations, and crescent shaped sparkling stretch of sand is to something die for. One can take a long stroll or the entire family can enjoy the beach parties in the shacks.

Things to do: Sunbathing, swimming, scuba diving, and dolphin spotting

Places to stay: The Tubki Resort, Art Resort Goa, and Marron Sea View Resort
