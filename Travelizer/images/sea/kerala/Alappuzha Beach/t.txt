If you want to spend some exclusive time with your family, Alappuzha Beach in Kerala is the place to be. Popularly known as the Venice of the East, this is a small piece of paradise on Earth with supreme scenic beauty and serenity. And some of the best beach resorts for families here ensure a wonderful holiday in Alappuzha.

Things to do: Kayaking and canoeing

Places to stay: Lemon Tree, Alleppey Beach Resorts, Vasundhara Sarovar Premiere, and Punnamada Resort
